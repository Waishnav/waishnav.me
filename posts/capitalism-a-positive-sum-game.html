Context

First lets understand what is positive-sum games?
It is a term in game theory that mean in any contest or competition, despite who wins or loses, everyone who participates gains something which is valuable Indirectly it means a total of wins and losses adds up to a positive value at the end.
In other words "A positive-sum occurs when resources are somehow increased and an approach is formulated in which the desires and needs of all concerned are satisfied. For this to happen, resources must increase during the process and then be distributed to everyone’s benefit."
In contrast, Zero-sum game is occurs when resources ends up to zero at the end of the game, it means their has to be looser to have winner. (one can gain only at the expense of another.)

What is Capitalism ?

Capitalism is the systyem where everybody can own something which is valueable. Its about private ownership. So lets understand why I said Capitalism is not zero sum game??
It is positive-sum game cause of the fact that market is open, its not moderated,also fact that everybody gets benefit whoever is involve in the trasaction to buy smth from company/creator, I'm talking about everyone here, leme justify for example guy living in capitalist society buys a car, the company who made that car gave wages to their employees, employees got benefit, the one who buys car get benefit of comfort and efficiently moving from one place to another, hence customer got benefited.
Note : he does have spend money to buy the car, this doesn't mean that at the consumer end its zero sum. People buys things only if people finds value in.
And the owner of the company got benefit from the profits that they earn through selling the cars. Hence capilism creates win-win situation for everyone who gets involve.
In the whole example, we see that money is just way to transfer wealth.

Flaws in Capitalism

There are lot of flaws of the capitalsm, one of its flaws is existance of monopoly, if any company end up becoming monopoly in the market, consumer suffers the most, cause that company can set any price of their product/service and there is no other company to serve the customer.
There are some other flaws but its not the topic of discussion.
